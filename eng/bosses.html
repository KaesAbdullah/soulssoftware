HONORABLE

BOSSES

SIR ALONNE

Sir Alonne is number 1 of the bosses. It is a great honor to battle against him, since the samurai himself bows before fighting to the death.

Aside from the epic battle, the music makes us feel that we are really fighting against someone honorable. Your body fills with energy just by listening to the soundtrack. All this, in addition to its sad lore.

GENICHIRO ASHINA

Genichiro Ashina is another of the great bosses of this saga, a samurai, like Sir Alonne, with whom we fight at the beginning and this one defeats us.

This defeat tells us that we are not ready yet to fight against someone so strong and gifted like Genichiro. He is only blinded with having the power of the divine heir.

GWYN

This boss, apart from being another of the honorable bosses, is a boss that haves a sad ending. Well we have to fight to the death against his body without conscience.

"The lord of cinder", as he is vaguely called. The one of himself nickname of him plus the sad soundtrack of him already tells us that he is just a soulless shell that died sacrificing itself to the flame.

GEHRMAN, THE FIRST HUNTER

Known as the first hunter, he is the creator of the base of all trick weapons in the game. He is tied against his will, to the hunter's dream.

At the end of the game he will let you choose between "getting rid" of the dream, letting him kill you. But if you reject the offer, you must fight to the death against him. That is, if or if you ended up dead at his hands.

ORNSTEIN

Also known as Dragon Slayer, it is a boss with whom we will fight to get one of the best objects in the game, this allows us to travel between bonfires.

But he is not going to make it so easy for us, since he is not alone, we will fight against him and his friend Smough. If we managed to kill his friend, he will get angry and absorb his power to take revenge.

ARTORIAS

Without a doubt, this is a great boss, it has a very sad story with a sad ending. As with Gwyn, we will fight his shell without conscience.

In his lore, he meets a gray wolf that he calls Sif, another boss we battle. Unfortunately, they had to separate due to a tragedy that occurred. Knowing this, killing his wolf makes our hearts twist.
